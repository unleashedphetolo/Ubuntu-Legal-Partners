Overview

Our practice areas cover a broad spectrum of legal services designed to address various client needs. In Family Law, we handle divorce and maintenance disputes, child custody and guardianship issues, and provide guidance on the legalities of customary marriages. Our Corporate and Commercial Law services include company registration and compliance, contract negotiations, and support for mergers and acquisitions. Within Labour and Employment Law, we offer representation for unfair dismissals, workplace discrimination cases, and union disputes. Property Law encompasses property transfers, lease agreements, and resolving land disputes. Lastly, our commitment to Human Rights and Public Interest Law is demonstrated through advocacy for marginalized communities, handling constitutional law cases, and providing pro bono legal assistance to those in need.
