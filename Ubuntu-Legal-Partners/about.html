About Us

Ubuntu Legal Partners, located in the heart of Cape Town, Western Cape, South Africa, embodies the spirit of justice and community through its guiding principle, Ubuntu. With the tagline "Justice for All, Rooted in Ubuntu," the firm is dedicated to delivering compassionate and accessible legal services while promoting fairness and equity across the region. The Cape Town setting, rich in culture and diversity, serves as the perfect backdrop for a firm committed to uplifting and empowering its community.

Our Mission

At Ubuntu Legal Partners, we strive to uphold justice and fairness, guided by the principles of Ubuntu – humanity and compassion in all that we do.

Meet Our Attorneys

Thandeka Mokoena

Thandeka Mokoena is the visionary behind Ubuntu Legal Partners. With an illustrious career spanning 18 years, Thandeka has become a trusted authority in family and human rights law. After earning her LLB from the University of Cape Town, she dedicated her early career to advocating for vulnerable communities, focusing on gender equality and children's rights.

Sipho Dlamini

Sipho Dlamini, a Corporate Law Expert at Ubuntu Legal Partners, has over 12 years of experience advising businesses on complex legal matters. He holds an LLM in Corporate Governance from Stellenbosch University and is a certified arbitrator.

Ayesha Khan

Ayesha Khan is an accomplished attorney with a decade of expertise in property and real estate law. After graduating with distinction from the University of Pretoria, Ayesha worked with some of South Africa’s top real estate developers, handling high-profile cases on property transfers and land disputes.

Johan van der Merwe

Johan van der Merwe brings 15 years of expertise in labour and employment law to Ubuntu Legal Partners. A graduate of the University of Stellenbosch, Johan is renowned for representing clients in high-stakes unfair dismissal and workplace discrimination cases.
