Award Winning Attorney

Thandeka Mokoena is the visionary behind Ubuntu Legal Partners. With an illustrious career spanning 18 years, Thandeka has become a trusted authority in family and human rights law. After earning her LLB from the University of Cape Town, she dedicated her early career to advocating for vulnerable communities, focusing on gender equality and children's rights. Her commitment to justice was cemented when she led groundbreaking cases addressing domestic abuse and child custody laws in South Africa. Beyond the courtroom, Thandeka is a mentor for aspiring young attorneys and frequently speaks at conferences on social justice and family law reform.
